Programs for Separated

or Divorcing Parents

 with Joyce Dolberg Rowe and Melissa Johnston

Court mandated Parent Education Program

for separated and divorcing parents.

 

 
1. Part I: Friday evening Nov 20, 6:00 PM- 8:30PM
Part II: Saturday morning Nov 21, 9:30 AM-12:00 PM
Location: 485 Nantasket Ave Unit A, Hull
 
Call the office for other upcoming dates and times.
(781) 925-3500
 
 

 

 

Our program will:

  • Satisfy the divorce court mandate for parents of minor children

  • Identify the emotional stages the parents will experience in the divorce process, while also addressing the needs of the children based upon their particular stage of development.

  • Address financial responsibility of both parents

  • Both positive and negative impact of parental behavior on the child and the need for positive communication skills

  • Lead to understanding the divorce process from the child's perspective: the feeling that the divorce is their fault, and the effect of the loss of extended family, home, school, camp, or friends on the child

  • Help the participants understand what it means to be emotionally available and involved, offering effective reassurance, and provide consistency for the children.

  • Address the deleterious effects for a child witnessing parents in conflict, introducing children to dating partners too soon, speaking poorly about the non-present parent, returning children late from visits or not having children ready for visits, using children as messengers or informants, and placing adult burdens on children

Grief Recovery Group

with Melissa Johnston

 

Call for Details

 

Location:  Hull

 

 

Grief is so much more than death and divorce...it is the loss of those hopes, dreams, expectations and trust that transcend every part of our lives.  This internationally acclaimed program is an effective way to heal from emotional and spiritual pain from all of life's losses. Grief is conflicting feelings caused by the end of or change in a familiar pattern of behavior.

 

These are some of the things we will discuss and learn about in the group:

 

* Grief: A Neglected and Misunderstood Process

* Academy Award Performances

* Identifying Short-Term-Energy Relievers

* Different, Better and More

* The Loss History Graph

* Introducing the Relationship Graph

* Apologies, Forgiveness, and Significant Statements 

* Completion Letter

 

With correct information and correct choices, a person can recover from any significant loss and find some healing and hope for today, and for the future.
